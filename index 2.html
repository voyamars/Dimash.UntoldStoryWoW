The Untold Story of a Great Artist

DIMASH.
UNTOLD STORY

A global literary journey into the heart of an artist.

Dimash.untold story brings together five authors from five continents to explore Dimash Kudaibergen beyond the stage — through stories, culture, and emotion.

This is not a biography.
This is a creative, cross-cultural portrait of an artist who connects the world through music — told from outside in, through voices shaped by different worlds but touched by one voice.

About The Book

Beyond The Spotlight

Five continents. Seven voices. One extraordinary artist.

This unique literary project brings together writers from around the globe to create an unprecedented portrait of Dimash Kudaibergen. Each author contributes their cultural perspective, creating a tapestry of stories that reveal the universal impact of music and artistry.


Through creative storytelling, cultural insights, and emotional depth, we explore how one voice can transcend borders, languages, and cultures to touch hearts worldwide.

The Authors' Journey

A Cultural Immersion Experience

1

Arrival in Almaty

The beginning of an extraordinary journey

  • Luxury stay at The Ritz-Carlton
  • Cultural welcome dinner and orientation
  • Private jeep tours to Kolsay Lake, Kaindy Lake, Charyn Canyon
2

Cultural Immersion

Deep dive into Kazakh culture

  • Meetings with Kazakh writers, musicians, and historians
  • Guided tours of museums, bazaars, and local art scenes
  • Optional folklore or yurt experience
3

Visit to Astana

Exploring modern Kazakhstan

  • Stay at Sheraton Astana
  • Explore modern Kazakhstan: EXPO city, Baiterek, National Museum
  • Sessions with local journalists and literary critics
4

Retreat to Burabay

Nature immersion for writing and reflection

  • 2-day nature immersion for writing and reflection
  • Lake views, forest walks, traditional cuisine
  • Creative workshops in natural setting
5

Journey to Aktobe

Dimash's hometown and roots

  • Visit Dimash's hometown and key childhood places
  • Conversations with family, teachers, and local fans
  • Explore the spiritual and emotional roots of his music
6

Return to Almaty (Final Phase)

The culmination of the journey

  • Stay in a private mountain resort
  • Three private creative sessions with Dimash
  • Final workshops, interviews, and writing reflections
7

Departure

The beginning of a new chapter

  • Authors return to their home countries
  • Begin writing their chapter, shaped by lived experience
  • Integration of cultural insights into literary work

Meet The Authors

Seven Languages, Seven Voices

Because Dimash's voice crossed borders long before this book did.

We've chosen seven languages that reflect where Dimash's artistry has created the deepest emotional and cultural impact. Each language opens a door to a dedicated audience — and a powerful market.

English (USA)

The global language of publishing and international recognition.

Hua Hsu

Acclaimed author of Stay True, a music‑centred memoir praised for its cultural insight and emotional depth. A credible, thoughtful voice ideal for capturing Dimash's cross‑cultural resonance.

Chinese (Simplified)

Where Dimash's worldwide journey began, with unmatched fan devotion.

Wang Ping

Shanghai-born, US-educated poet, novelist, and cultural critic. Known for her multimedia storytelling and cross-cultural themes. Published internationally, and likely responsive to well-defined projects.

Russian

A close neighbor and cultural ally with a deeply loyal following.

Sergey Sosedov

Moscow-based music journalist and critic, longtime columnist for reputable outlets, and known for TV judging roles. Engaged in music discourse and accessible to commissioning.

Kazakh

His homeland. His soul. The origin of the story.

Aigul Kemelbayeva

Awarded Kazakh writer, critic, and essayist published in Kazakh and Russian. Actively engaged in literary circles with strong cultural insight.

Turkish

A natural cultural bridge, with growing regional admiration.

Elif Şafak

Respected Istanbul-based author of literary fiction and essays on culture, identity, and the East‑West dynamic. While well-known, her profile is suited for collaboration with clear creative and cultural parameters.

Spanish (Latin America/Spain)

Latin America and Spain form one of Dimash's most passionate global fanbases.

Andrea Chapela

Mexican writer of award-winning essays and fiction; her work spans narrative nonfiction and cultural themes. Published, yet accessible and ideal for commissioned projects.

Japanese

A refined audience that values artistry, discipline, and emotion.

Masanori Ito

Prominent music critic and radio host in Japan, known for his deep engagement with music journalism. Not a celebrity author, but a respected voice in Japan's music culture.
